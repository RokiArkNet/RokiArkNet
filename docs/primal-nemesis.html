Getting Started

Focus on building a core crafting base, preparing for early hunts, and securing the resources required for future tiers.

Core Crafting Structures

Secure a safe starting base — land or raft — and prioritize these essential structures:

Primal Forge
Smelts metal and alloys. Crafts advanced ingots (Copper, Tin). Required for Zinc Ore.
Primal Cauldron
Brews custom kibbles, potions, dyes, and consumables.
Primal Bench
Primary station for modded gear, kibble, and boss item crafting.

Loot System Overview

All loot in Primal Nemesis rolls randomized stat bonuses — armor, weapons, tools, saddles, rings, and dino totems.

  • Loot Bosses
  • Quests
  • Wild dino kills

Higher tiers provide more stat slots and stronger values. Loot quality ranges from Common through Ancient. See the Loot System section for full details.

Alpha Tier

Alpha-tier creatures mark your first major power spike. Their proximity buff increases damage and resistance for nearby dinos.

Structure Damage — Wood

Gear Up

Prepare a basic combat kit before engaging Alphas:

Weapons
Primal Spear, Bow, Tranq Arrows
Utility
Primal Parachute, Primal Grappling Gun
Alpha Blood
Used to craft Alpha-tier tranq ammo
Alpha Hide
Used for essential items such as the Primal Bed

First Tames

Once Alpha Kibble is available from egg farms, prioritize core utility tames:

  • Flyers — Pteranodons for travel and scouting
  • Gatherers — Parasaurs, Trikes, or Stegos for berry farming
Alpha tames provide the Alpha Buff — doubles damage and resistance for all nearby tamed dinos. Keep one nearby during any boss fight.

Recommended DPS Tames

Bring dedicated combat dinos for boss fights — Rexes, Spinos, or other high-damage Alpha-tier fighters. Focus on high health, high melee, good saddles, and Alpha Health Potions. Utility tames should stay at base.

The Alpha Boss

Primary progression gate of the Alpha tier. Defeating it unlocks Apex Kibble and the Primal Incubator.

Preparation

  • Craft the summon at the Primal Bench
  • Use a secure, enclosed arena
  • Bring saddled dinos and Alpha Health Potions

Phases

  • Phase 1 — Defeat the Alpha Boss
  • Phase 2 — Immediate respawn as Skeletal Alpha Boss. Both phases required for full rewards.

Rewards

Alpha Boss Skull Unlocks Apex Kibble Unlocks Primal Incubator

Skeletal & Boss Variants

Skeletal variants are stronger than normal counterparts and require Alpha/Apex Boss Kibble to tame. They drop Keratin and Blood when harvested.

Boss variants are stronger still, drop Element, and can be bred. Alpha Bosses require Alpha Boss Kibble; Apex Bosses require Apex Boss Kibble.

Apex Tier

After mastering Alpha, progression moves into the Apex tier. Apex creatures provide increased strength and durability.

Structure Damage — Stone

Bloodrage Mechanic

Apex creatures gain Bloodrage during combat.

  • Each kill increases damage output
  • Provides health regeneration over time

Bloodrage scales Apex dinos in sustained combat and multi-target encounters.

Advancing to Apex

  • Target Rexes, Spinos, and other high-DPS Apex dinos
  • Replace weaker Alpha tames with higher-stat Apex variants
The Apex Troll

First boss encounter in this tier — the next progression gate. Defeating it unlocks Elemental Kibble.

Boss fights apply a Grounded Debuff — disables flight, abilities, and mounting. No fliers.

Apex Feather Farm

Establish a reliable source of Apex Feathers before progressing — required for mid- to late-game crafting and kibble.

  • Argentavis, Dodo, Terror Bird, Archaeopteryx
  • Apex Dodos are the easiest starting option
Elemental Tier

After defeating the Apex Troll, you unlock Elemental Kibble. The Elemental tier revolves around exploiting strengths and weaknesses between types — team composition is critical.

Structure Damage — Stone
Any Elemental Blood can be used interchangeably — blood from any elemental type satisfies requirements calling for "Elemental Blood."

Elemental Kibble

Elemental creatures drop their respective cores (e.g., Volcanic Core, Hydro Core). In the Primal Cauldron: 1 Core → 10 Essence. Elemental Essence crafts Elemental Kibble.

  • Earth + Nature — Faster crafting + improved crop production
  • Frost + Volcanic — Full temperature control across all environments
Nature dinos provide a 150% greenhouse effect that REPLACES existing greenhouse bonuses rather than stacking. Do not use near a full greenhouse (300%) — it will reduce efficiency.
Elemental Kaiju Bosses

Can be crafted at the Primal Bench and challenged in any order. Each fight requires proper preparation.

Preparation

  • Fully saddled and healed tames
  • Stock Alpha and Nature Potions
  • Build a team of evolved Titans with high Health and Melee

Strategy

  • Prioritize Health and Melee for consistent DPS
  • Build a balanced team to counter elemental matchups
  • Use Alpha proximity buffs to enhance Elemental performance

Rewards

Boss Skull Elemental Fruit Seeds Elemental Boss Essence
Plasma / Ghost / Plague

The next trio of Nemesis tiers introduce corruption effects, unstable energy forms, and powerful spectral creatures.

Structure Damage — Stone

These three tiers form an internal interaction triangle:

  • Plague beats Plasma
  • Plasma beats Ghost
  • Ghost beats Plague
Plasma Immune to Projectiles ▼

Creatures composed of unstable plasma energy. Plasma beats Ghost. Immune to projectiles. Weak against Plague.

New Tools

  • Plasma Netgun — Used for trapping Ghosts
  • GhostBuster 9000 — Plasma-based KO tool
Plasma Boss

Beware the shield — keep your distance and dodge all incoming attacks. Can summon Minions similar to the vanilla Overseer fight.

Huge health pool. Come fully stocked on potions.
Ghost Nighttime Advantage ▼

Spectral aquatic creatures with powerful nighttime advantages. Immune to projectiles and normal tranqs. Can fly out of water. Extremely strong at night.

Taming Requirements

  • Plasma Netgun (to trap)
  • GhostBuster 9000 (to KO)
Ghost Boss — Flyers Only

Must be fought with flying creatures. Ground tames are not viable. Weak to Plasma. Ghosts are strongest at night.

Non-flyer strategies will fail. Bring strong fliers — the boss has a large health pool.
Plague Contact Virus ▼

Creatures influenced by virulent corruption. Plague beats Plasma. Plague creatures spread a virus on contact — applies a short-duration damage-over-time debuff.

Plague Boss
Unlocks Bionic Kibble
Bionic Tier

One of the most powerful stages in Primal Nemesis.

Structure Damage — Stone
Bionic Progression — Boss Chain

Defeat bosses to acquire both Bionic Skulls. Then craft the Bionic God Scroll at the Primal Bench to unlock the final Bionic Boss — the Scion of Darkness.

Victory Conditions — Scion of Darkness / Bionic God

  • High DPS for the boss phase
  • Fast tower-breakers for the shield phase
  • Strong mobility to dodge plasma fire
This is a multi-phase fight. Victory marks mastery of the Bionic Tier.

Rewards

Bionic Boss Skull Bionic Boss Essence Unlocks Primal Bionic Forge

Primal Bionic Forge

Unlocked by defeating the Bionic God. The only station that can refine:

  • Mithril Ore → Mithril Ingots
  • Gold Ore → Gold Ingots

Power Requirements: Storm Essence, Volcanic Essence, or a nearby Storm creature.

Celestial / Demonic

With Elemental and Bionic conquered, your next leap is into the Demonic and Celestial tiers.

Structure Damage — Tek (AoE attacks)

The Hunt for Honey

To craft Demonic and Celestial Kibble, hunt Demonic Bees and Celestial Bees in the wild.

These bees hit hard — bring your best gear or prepare to respawn.
Required Gear
Strong Bionic-tier tame, Fabricated Sniper Rifle, High-tier Bionic+ Tranqs
Two Paths
Full Assault (kill directly) or Tranq and Finish. Both yield 2 Honey per bee.

Tame Your First Bees

KO and tame Demonic and Celestial Bees. Once tamed, convert them into a Beehive via radial menu and insert Celestial or Demonic Flowers for passive honey production.

Tame as many variants as possible — each has unique abilities, and some are game-changers.
Demonic & Celestial Bosses

Prep

  • Bring strongest Bionic/Demonic/Celestial creatures with high-quality saddles and imprints
  • Stockpile Alpha Health Potions and Nature Buffs
  • Use Alpha Buffs mid-fight to tip the balance

Rewards

Demonic / Celestial Boss Skull Boss Essence Celestial & Demonic Seeds (for Godly Kibble)
Godly Tier

The final step in Nemesis progression — a culmination of your power, tames, and strategy. Health potions recommended due to high stats and projectile damage.

Godly Bosses

Six Godly Bosses roam the map — only one of each at a time. You'll be notified when one spawns. On death, each boss summons a tameable version of itself.

Nemesis — The Godly Boss

Collect tokens from all six Godly Bosses to summon Nemesis — the ultimate early-game fight designed to test everything you've learned. Conquer it to complete the early-game arc.

Rewards

Godly Potions Max XP Potions Godly Boss Essence Godly Boss Skull
Boss Reference

Loot Bosses

Rare enemies that spawn naturally in the wild across all tiers. Project a red beam into the sky — when attacked, the beam disappears to signal engagement. Drops go into inventory, not on the ground.

Saddles

Crafted saddles are always Primitive. Loot bosses are the primary source of higher-quality saddles with randomized stats. Can drop both finished saddles and blueprints.

Tame Helpers / Potions

Basic Tame Helper (x10) can be crafted in the Primal Cauldron. Stronger versions are exclusive loot boss drops. Alpha and Nature potions craftable in Cauldron; Plasma, Celestial, and Godly potions are exclusive drops.

Quests

  • Common Resource Quests — Triggered by killing creatures. Focused on farming basic resources.
  • High-Level Quests — Dropped by Imposter Loot Bosses (Alpha, Apex, Elemental, Bionic). Late-game progression content.
Quest scrolls must be consumed from a dino's inventory. Quest UI is only visible and usable while riding that specific dino.

Resource Ores
Nickel Ore Source: Resource Rock Golem ▼
  • Hover above a Resource Rock Golem with a flying mount to bait it into spawning Minions
  • Defeat and harvest Minions using a Modded Dino (Apex Rex recommended)
  • Kill and harvest the Golem itself — Modded Tools are bugged, use Modded Dinos
Look for Resource Rock Golems that show "Can be tamed with Modded Ore" — tame one and use its ability to spawn your own Minions for unlimited Nickel.

Taming the Golem

  • Griffin hover + shoot straight down with Rocket Launcher (aim for the head)
  • Or trap and use a Cannon for Torpor
  • Captain Reptile's Cannon mod can help
Starfire Metal / Mithril / Gold Source: DemiGod StarFire Golem ▼

When fighting a Resource Rock Golem, there is a 25% chance it respawns as a DemiGod StarFire Golem. During the fight it periodically summons meteors — harvest these for Starfire Metal, Mithril Ore, and Gold Ore. Each meteor lasts 7 minutes.

The meteor ability fires every 3 minutes. Keep the DemiGod alive to farm multiple waves — the most efficient method for gathering these ores.

Refining

Mithril and Gold Ore can only be smelted in the Primal Bionic Forge. Requires Storm Essence, Volcanic Essence, or a nearby Storm creature.

Rice / Kelp / Sushi

Use a Berry Gatherer (Trike, Bronto, Parasaur) to collect Rice and Seaweed seeds from Berry Bushes. Plant in greenhouse. For Prime Fish Meat, target Basilosaurus, Deinosuchus, Mosasaurus, or Spine Croc with Pickaxe or Chainsaw.

Prime Fish Meat does NOT stack — plan inventory space accordingly.

Combine Prime Fish Meat, Rice, and Kelp in the Primal Cauldron. Cauldron holds 50 items max.

Charms & Totems

All enhancers roll a value within a range when obtained — two items of the same type can have different strength levels.

Player Ring System

Rings are modular equipment with insertable charms. Ring tiers: Common → Uncommon → Rare → Epic → Legendary → Godly. Common rings start with 1 charm slot; each tier adds capacity.

Elemental Titans

How to Tame a Titan

  1. Defeat the Elemental Boss — Unlocks the Elemental Incubator Engram and drops the Boss Skull (required for Whelpling recipe)
  2. Hunt a Wild Elemental Youngling — Cannot be crafted or summoned. On kill, automatically drops an Elemental Heart into your inventory
  3. Place Incubator strategically — Keep one at base for recipes (DO NOT hatch from it) and one in an open area for the actual hatch
  4. Craft the Whelpling — Spawns immediately as a wild creature. KO and feed matching Elemental Kibble
Younglings hit HARDER than Elemental Bosses. Come fully geared with your best tames. Do NOT summon a Whelpling inside your base — it will destroy structures.

Titan Evolution

  • Whelpling — Baby/juvenile version
  • Youngling — Use evolution item in inventory. Similar strength to wild variant
  • Adult Titan — Gains ALL special attacks from boss variant. Ideal for Elemental boss fights
FAQ & Player Tips
How do dinos spawn? ▼
Modded creatures "piggyback" from vanilla dino spawns. A vanilla Argent spawning might result in an Alpha Argent or Apex Argent instead.
How do I get unfertilized eggs for kibble? ▼
  1. Set layegginterval to 0.3 in GAME INI
  2. Ensure dinos are mate boosted (1 male + females)
  3. Disable mating (unfertilized eggs needed)
  4. Use an OviRaptor with egg-boost buff
  5. Stay in render range unless using a mod
Why aren't modded dinos spawning? ▼
Move the mod to 1st/top of mod order, then perform a dino wipe.
Can we take modded dinos into boss arenas? ▼
If their vanilla counterpart can enter, the modded version can also. Note: Due to size increases at higher tiers, some dinos become too large to enter the Tek Cave (e.g., Elemental Rexes).
What are Colossal & Pygmy traits? ▼
Both have faster movement and increased melee damage.

Colossal — Higher base health and melee. Suited for tanking.
Pygmy — 10% chance to dodge incoming attacks. Effective in long fights.

Can be applied via trait items or appear randomly through breeding.
What are the new dino traits? ▼
Nuclear — Builds an internal timer when aggressive or taking damage. Detonates in a nuclear explosion at zero. When tamed, triggers below 10% health.

Volatile — Explodes on death. Both wild and tamed versions.

Nightmare — Doubled stats during nighttime.

Nighthunter — Extremely aggressive at night with increased aggro range.

Chunky — Increased harvest health; yields more materials when harvested.
How does the quality ranking system work? ▼
Common → Uncommon → Rare → Epic → Legendary → Divine → Ancient

Divine is the new high-end tier above Legendary. Ancient is only obtainable through quests.
