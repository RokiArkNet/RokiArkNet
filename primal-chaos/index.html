    Primal Chaos — Guide Content¶

    baseline¶

    All stats based on Level 150 unboosted creatures — no server multipliers or custom imprints. Assumes working knowledge of basic ARK mechanics.

    tier-list¶

    Toxic/Beta | Alpha | Electric/Volcanic/Hydro | Shadow/Fairy | Mythic/Fabled/Legendary | Angelic/Demonic | Origin | Spirit/Chaos | Nightmare

    getting-started¶

    Survive long enough to establish a safe location — whether it's a raft base or a small land outpost tucked away from danger.

    Early Essentials to Craft¶

    Primal Forge — For smelting modded ores and materials. Crafted in inventory.

    Primal Smithy — For crafting weapons, armor, and survival tools. Crafted in inventory.

    WARNING: Avoid glowing-eyed creatures early on — they usually belong to higher tiers and will demolish you if provoked.

    Build Your Food & Egg Supply¶

    Before taming your first modded creatures, you'll need a steady supply of vanilla crops and eggs.

    Crops to Grow: Rockarrot, Savoroot, Citronal, Longrass

    Egg Layers to Tame: Dodos, Dilos, other common early-game passive layers

    See the FAQ section for optimizing egg production.

    toxic-alpha¶

    Your first real power spike comes from Toxic-tier creatures. These tames are the gateway to stronger knockout tools and your next tier of progression.

    STRUCTURE DAMAGE: N/A

    Taming Toxic Creatures¶

    Weapons — Basic Tranq Arrows or a bow

    Feed — Toxic Kibble after knockout

    Why Tame? — Excellent early resource gathering and mobility. Toxic Blood is essential for crafting Potent Tranq Arrows.

    Kill a few extra Toxic creatures to stockpile Toxic Blood for crafting.

    Crafting Alpha Kibble¶

    Once you have Toxic creatures breeding, prepare for Alpha-tier taming.

    Alpha Kibble Recipe — Requires unfertilized Toxic Eggs. Combine with vanilla ingredients in the Primal Smithy.

    Best Early Breeders — Toxic Dodos, Toxic Dilos, Toxic Parasaurs

    Keep multiple females with one male in a fenced pen for constant egg production. An Egg Collector makes this step far easier.

    Alpha Ascent¶

    With Alpha Kibble in hand, upgrade your tames and set up for the Elemental tier.

    How to Tame Alpha — Knock out Alpha-tier dinos using Potent Tranqs or better. Feed them Alpha Kibble.

    Why Kill Some? — Defeating Alphas drops Alpha Blood — essential for crafting stronger tranqs like Alpha Arrows and Alpha Bullets.

    Focus on utility creatures first — harvesters, tanks, and strong early fighters.

    elemental¶

    With Elemental Kibble ready, build your elemental army and push into the first real boss fights of Primal Chaos.

    STRUCTURE DAMAGE: N/A

    Elemental Matchups¶

    Hydro — Strong vs Volcanic | Weak to Electric | Gains buffs near water, suffocates enemy riders and dinos in close range

    Volcanic — Strong vs Electric | Weak to Hydro | Applies the Enflamed debuff, dealing burn damage over time; great for sustained DPS

    Electric — Strong vs Hydro | Weak to Volcanic | Moves quickly and can stun targets; excellent for mobility and crowd control

    Aim to tame at least one of each type to prepare for bosses and counter a variety of threats.

    Elemental Bosses¶

    WARNING: No fliers — they will be grounded on attack.

    How to Summon — Elemental Scrolls at the Primal Smithy or find them roaming wild

    Boss Drops — Elemental Seeds (for rare crops), Tameable Tokens (20% drop, at least one guaranteed), Elemental Saddles

    Bring multiple tames, healing potions, and be ready for sustained fights.

    Growing Elemental Crops¶

    1. Plant Elemental Seeds dropped by bosses in crop plots
    2. Harvest Elemental Fruits to craft Shadow and Fairy Kibble

    shadow-fairy¶

    The Shadow and Fairy tiers mark your entry into a more tactical stage of progression. Both are essential for unlocking the next three kibble tiers.

    STRUCTURE DAMAGE: N/A

    Fairy Creatures¶

    Strong lifesteal and regeneration abilities. Excellent for sustained fights and team support.

    Shadow Creatures¶

    Passive reflect ability bounces damage back at attackers. Excellent against hard-hitting enemies.

    Tame both types to ensure steady egg production for advanced kibble tiers.

    Crafting Mythic, Fabled & Legendary Kibble¶

    Once your Shadow and Fairy breeders are producing eggs, you can craft:

    Mythic Kibble — For Mythic-tier tames

    Fabled Kibble — For Fabled-tier tames

    Legendary Kibble — For Legendary-tier tames

    mythic-fabled-legendary¶

    You've entered the mid-game trifecta — three powerful sub-tiers that share the same level but offer different specialties.

    STRUCTURE DAMAGE: N/A

    Mythic¶

    Excellent gatherers with 3x harvest bonus on most resources.

    Legendary¶

    Combat-focused with stacking kills, natural regen, bleed, and rider protection.

    Fabled¶

    Utility-oriented with high base weight — ideal for hauling and crafting support.

    Use these three sub-tiers to fill gaps in your lineup — gatherers, combat specialists, and heavy haulers.

    Preparing for Demonic & Angelic¶

    Breed and imprint your best Mythic, Legendary, and Fabled tames for maximum stats.

    Stockpile resources — Demonic and Angelic progression requires advanced crafting materials.

    Farm boss tokens & rare drops — Essential for summoning higher-tier encounters later.

    Upgrade saddles — Quality gear is critical for surviving Demonic/Angelic fights.

    Prepare high-tier tranq ammo — These tames require significant torpor to knock out.

    Deprecated Items¶

    WARNING: Some items in the Chaos Smithy are deprecated. Primal Polymer and Primal Electronics no longer drop. The Demonic Rocket Launcher engram does not function.

    demonic-angelic¶

    Once you've unlocked Demonic and Angelic Kibble, tame these two powerful opposites and begin the climb toward the Origin Bosses.

    STRUCTURE DAMAGE: N/A

    Demonic Creatures¶

    High burst damage. Excellent for aggressive offense and crowd control.

    Angelic Creatures¶

    Focused on healing, support, and sustain. Often boost nearby allies.

    These tames complement each other in battle — one strikes hard, the other keeps your team alive.

    Berserk Mode¶

    Demonic Berserk — Boosts Speed, Torpor Output, Damage, AOE Damage. Sets nearby enemies on fire.

    Angelic Berserk — Boosts Speed, Torpor Output, Health Regen, Max Health. Activates Allied Damage Absorption (redirects ALL incoming damage from nearby tames to the Angelic).

    Activation — PC: Press X | Console: Click Right Thumbstick

    WARNING: Wild Demonics auto-activate Berserk Mode below 40% health, doubling their torpor.

    Hunt Primal Creatures¶

    To advance, you'll need Primal Blood — a key ingredient for crafting Origin Tokens.

    Only true Primal creatures drop Primal Blood — No drops from Tek or Resurrected variants.

    WARNING: Primal creatures apply a Grounded Debuff — flying is completely disabled.

    Defeat Origin Creatures¶

    Slaying Origin creatures grants Origin Tokens needed to summon Demonic and Angelic bosses.

    Each Origin Kill Rewards — Origin Token, High-tier materials

    WARNING: These fights are tough — bring fully imprinted, buffed Demonic or Angelic tames with quality saddles and healing potions.

    Boss: Skorvax the Demonking¶

    Demonic Boss summoned using Origin Tokens.

    Drops — Chance for Tameable Token, Saddle (craftable in Primal Smithy), higher quality saddles from Ascendant Lootboss

    Boss: Draconis (Origin Boss)¶

    The most powerful Origin Boss, summoned with crafted scrolls at the Primal Smithy.

    Phase Mechanics — Activates invulnerable shield at ~90%, ~50%, and ~10% HP. Kill summoned minions to break shields.

    Tameable Draconis — Chance to drop Tameable Token. Requires Origin Artifact from Mr. Artifact the Original.

    Dodoreapers take 80% reduced damage unless affected by Charged Light — bring a Glowtail or other charge pet.

    spirit-chaos¶

    Spirit and Chaos creatures represent the pinnacle of standard taming progression. These powerful tames unlock your path to Origins and beyond.

    STRUCTURE DAMAGE: N/A

    Tame Spirit & Chaos Creatures¶

    Defeat the Spirit and Chaos bosses (summoned at the Primal Smithy) to unlock the ability to tame their wild counterparts. Each boss unlocks their respective kibble engram.

    These are among the strongest creatures in the game. Build your final boss team around them.

    Spirit Creatures¶

    High damage output with excellent mobility. Spirit tames excel at quick strikes and repositioning.

    Chaos Creatures¶

    Devastating AOE attacks and crowd control. Chaos tames dominate in prolonged fights against multiple enemies.

    Use Spirit and Chaos kibble to prepare for Origin encounters.

    origins¶

    Origins are elite-tier creatures that bridge the gap between standard progression and the ultimate Nightmare tier.

    STRUCTURE DAMAGE: N/A

    Taming Origins¶

    Tameable Origins can only be summoned using Tameable Tokens, dropped by Draconis minions (50% chance).

    Taming Process:

    1. Weaken to 20% health before torpor will affect them
    2. Keep them below 20% until fully knocked out (they heal quickly)
    3. Requires an Origin Artifact to summon (dropped by Mr. Artifact the Original)

    Origin Passive Buffs¶

    Self — 30% resistance and 10% healing

    Allies — 10% resistance and 5% healing

    Origins are powerful support tames that buff your entire army.

    nightmares¶

    Nightmares represent the ultimate tier of Primal Chaos — devastating creatures that can challenge even the Deathknight.

    STRUCTURE DAMAGE: N/A

    Hunt Primal Resurrected Creatures¶

    Powerful untamable enemies designed to test endgame strength.

    Main Drop — Primal Bones (used to craft Nightmares and Deathknights)

    WARNING: Grounded Debuff — fliers disabled. Extremely high damage.

    Nightmare Bosses¶

    Required for unlocking the Deathknight and obtaining Nightmare Tokens.

    How to Encounter — Summon using crafted Nightmare Summons (Origin Tokens + Primal Bones) or rare wild spawns.

    Drops — Nightmare Token (required for Deathknight), high-end tools, armor, and rare mats.

    Evolving Origins to Nightmares¶

    Craft the Nightmare Evolution item inside the Origin's inventory.

    Required Materials — Angelic Soul x5, Demonic Soul x5, Nightmare Token x1, Origin Token x1, Primal Blood x300, Primal Soul x1, Tameable Origin Summon x1, Chaos Kibble x1, Fabled Blood x500, Origin Blood x5, Origin Soul x1, Primal Bones x100, Spirit Kibble x1

    WARNING: Your Origin will lose its resistance and healing passives, but gain even greater power as a Nightmare!

    Boss: Deathknight¶

    The final challenge of Primal Chaos progression.

    Summon — Crafted using Nightmare Tokens + rare materials

    Fight Mechanics — Takes reduced damage from all sources. Nightmares are your key DPS; Angelics in Berserk Mode can soak massive hits with damage absorption.

    Drops — DK Armor (unbreakable), DK Saddle, Demonic Rocket Launcher (novelty), Tameable Token (10% chance)

    Enter this fight with coordination, healing, and max-tier gear — every step of your journey leads to this moment.

    loot-bosses¶

    Loot Bosses provide progressively better rewards as they evolve through tiers.

    Evolution Mechanic¶

    Kill a Loot Boss to guarantee its evolution. The upgraded version spawns near the dead body and will be passive (unless you stand right next to it).

    Persistence — Maximum of 6 Loot Bosses can exist on the map at once.

    Drops — Only Loot Bosses drop saddles. Drops scale upward — each higher tier inherits the saddle drop pool of all tiers below it.

    Loot Boss Tiers¶

    Ramshackle — Spawns naturally in the wild. Kill Mesopithecus and Procoptodon to encourage spawns.

    Apprentice — Evolved from Ramshackle

    Journeyman — Evolved from Apprentice

    Mastercraft — Evolved from Journeyman

    Ascendant — Drops Demonic and Angelic Saddles

    Origin — Goes berserk randomly during combat. Drops quality Origin Saddles.

    Nightmare — Goes berserk randomly during combat. Drops quality Nightmare Saddles.

    special-encounters¶

    Mr. Artifact the Stronger¶

    Basic version. Drops useful artifacts. Great early game option for gathering boss requirements.

    Location — Found anywhere Megatheriums spawn. Kill megatheriums to encourage spawns.

    Persistence — Max 6 Mr. Artifacts will persistently exist on the map.

    Mr. Artifact the Original¶

    30% chance to spawn when you defeat a regular Mr. Artifact.

    Drops — More artifacts and high chance to drop Origin Artifact blueprint.

    Origin Artifact — Used to tame Origins.

    WARNING: Has punishing attacks — requires careful dodging and observation.

    faq¶

    How do dinos spawn? Modded creatures piggyback from vanilla dino spawns. A vanilla Argent spawning might result in a Beta Argent or Alpha Argent instead.

    How do I get unfertilized eggs for kibble? Set layegginterval to 0.3 in GAME INI. Ensure dinos are mate boosted (1 male + females). Disable mating (unfertilized eggs needed). Use an OviRaptor for egg-laying boost.

    Why aren't dinos spawning? Move the mod to 1st/top of mod order, then perform a dino wipe.

    Can we take modded dinos into boss arenas? If their vanilla counterpart can enter, the modded version also can. Note: Due to size changes at higher tiers, some dinos become too large to enter the Tek Cave.

    How do we unlock tek engrams or get element? Primal Chaos doesn't provide custom ways — use vanilla Ark mechanics.

    Elemental Titans? Removed from the mod indefinitely.

    Omega Dinos? Removed from the mod indefinitely.

    Tool Harvesting Multipliers¶

    Mythic Tools — 3x harvesting

    Elemental Tools — 2.5x harvesting

    Alpha Tools — 2x harvesting

    Toxic Tools — 1.5x harvesting

    Tranq Ammo Values¶

    Tranq Darts (Longneck) — Potent 880-1.9k | Alpha 1.7k-3.8k | Elemental 2.6k-6.4k | Mythic 5.5k-9.2k

    Tranq Bullets (Sniper) — Potent 2k | Alpha 4.5k | Elemental 8.5k | Mythic 13k | Primal 20k (boss drop only)

    Compound Tranq Arrows — Potent 3k | Alpha 6k | Elemental 12k | Mythic 17k | Primal 30k (boss drop only)
