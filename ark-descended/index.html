    Ark Descended — Guide Content¶

    baseline¶

    All testing, stat values, and balance notes in this guide are based on Level 150 creatures with unboosted stats (no server stat multipliers or custom imprints). Damage and health scaling will vary on boosted servers or higher-level wilds.

    tier-list¶

    T1 Ordinary/Descended | T2 Excellent | T3 Divine/Devilish | T4 Celestial/Abyssal | T5 Warchiefs | T6 Bionic | T7 Wardens | T8 God Trials | T9 Dark Seals | T10 Dream

    getting-started¶

    Focus on taming Ordinary and Descended creatures, building your Passive Production farm, and preparing for the Excellent Essence Boss (Excellent Dodo).

    STRUCTURE DAMAGE: N/A

    T1: Ordinary & Descended¶

    Craft the Descend Smithy first — it's the cornerstone of Ark Descended's progression.

    Use it to make Descended Tools (5x harvest rate) and the Tranq Pike, then spec into melee damage to improve knockout power.

    Early Gathering Tames:

    Miner Anky — 5x resource harvesting, auto-smelts metal

    Descended Pelagornis — Best source of organic polymer early on

    Logger Castoroides — Harvests tremendous amounts of wood

    Early Farming & Potion Prep¶

    Get crop plots running early — veggies and kibble ingredients power later crafting. Stock Hunger Potions — they force tames to eat faster.

    Ordinary and Descended creatures make up your starting roster. They tame with vanilla foods and vanilla kibble.

    Ordinary vs. Descended:

    Ordinary dinos — 2x the stats of their normal forms, providing sturdier baseline tames

    Descended dinos — Vary in strength and utility, bridging the path to higher tiers

    See the Special Taming Mechanics section for creatures that require unique mechanics to tame.

    Early Production Setup¶

    All Descended dinos passively produce Descended Essence — a key resource throughout progression. Some produce extra resources.

    Descended Dodo and Bob (Descended Phiomia) are easy starter producers.

    All creatures in T1 (and most everything in T2) use vanilla saddles.

    Descended lightpets grant permanent 10% damage boost and 10% damage resistance to your mount (not survivor). This "Critter Bonus" scales across all tiers.

    Health Potions can be used both from your hotbar and directly inside your dino's inventory — alternating between the two helps bypass potion cooldowns.

    Descended Griffin or Ordinary Snow Owl make great early fliers for scouting.

    First Boss Prep¶

    Your T1 creatures form the army for the Excellent Essence Boss — the first progression gate.

    Descended Phoenix — Strong starter tame with Extraordinary Kibble. Excellent against early bosses.

    t2-excellent¶

    STRUCTURE DAMAGE: N/A

    Excellent Essence Boss¶

    The Excellent Essence Boss (Excellent Dodo) introduces mid-tier combat and passive production.

    Defeating it spawns a tameable Excellent Dodo (passive tame). It produces Excellent Essence and Rare Flowers.

    Notable Rewards:

    Excellent Essence — Required for crafting Tier 2 and Tier 3 items

    Excellent Dodo — Passive producer unlocked after the fight

    Excellent Chibi — Grants +100 Stamina and +100 HP regenerated per second for your dino while equipped

    Excellent Flak Armor — Durable armor set for Tier 2 exploration and combat

    Excellent Tier Dinos¶

    Once able, breed and level Excellent creatures heavily. These are your main force for taking on the bosses ahead.

    With access to Excellent Kibble, you can now tame a Descended DodoWyvern — a powerful, mobile flier perfect for the T1 Loot Boss and Unreal Boss fights.

    Once tamed, your DodoWyvern opens the path to new opportunities: use it to safely tame a Descended Rock Drake, allowing you to obtain your very own Descended Reaper Prince or Princess.

    Unlocking the War Generator¶

    Craft the War Generator with Excellent Essence — it's your boss summoning hub from T3 onward.

    Combat Bosses — Spider, Gorilla & Dragon¶

    Combat Bosses are empowered versions of vanilla Spider, Gorilla, and Dragon.

    Combat Boss Rules:

    • Can only be damaged by Ordinary, Descended, or Excellent dinos
    • Excellent Boss works but does reduced damage
    • Damage scales with your tames' HP, armor, and drag weight — stacking pure tank stats can make them hit harder
    • Defeating any Combat Boss unlocks the Unreal tier

    Recommended Strategy: Kill Spider first with Therizino packs (3-4 groups of 6-8) — sustained damage and bleed overwhelm it.

    Alternatively, Charge-Adapted Reaper offers safer solo play — ranged acid + energy shield. Keep a light pet active.

    Key Drops:

    Green Tribute — Spider

    Blue Tribute — Gorilla

    Red Tribute — Dragon

    Combat Loot Box — Combat Essence (from all three bosses)

    These tributes and essences are required for summoning and crafting later-tier bosses.

    Same strategy for Gorilla — avoid rock throw (30s cooldown). Return to Dragon after Sin of Power; any Unreal Excellent Boss finishes the trio easily.

    With Combat Essence, you can now summon a Mek from the War Generator — defeat it to obtain its Mek Blueprint.

    Unreal Tier — Harnessing the Power of Nightmares¶

    Combat Bosses unlock Unreal Kibble for Unreal Dinos. They have the Unreal Debuff — constant health drain negated by Descended Milk.

    Unreal creatures offer:

    • Passive production of Unreal Essence, essential for Excellent Boss Kibble
    • Passive Unreal ability: if enemy attacks miss, they begin to regenerate health

    Craft Unreal Armor for your Unreal tames — significantly increases survivability for farming.

    You'll need BioToxin for Excellent Tranq Arrows — tame a BioToxin Bug.

    The Unreal Megalodon is among the most useful Unreal Dinos — it can swim and fly!

    Excellent Bosses¶

    Excellent Bosses (start with Indoms or Gigas) use the Torpid Activate system:

    • Can only take torpor once health drops below 50%
    • Every torpor hit triggers a Torpid Activate timer — reapply torpor before it expires or progress resets
    • Once knocked out, you have ~10 seconds to place Excellent Boss Kibble and use a Hunger Potion

    Recommended Strategy: Kite into deep water — slows movement and gives room to reposition.

    Notable Rewards:

    Boss Orbs — Key ingredient for crafting more Excellent Boss Kibble

    These creatures become your backbone for tackling the Three Sins and beyond.

    Unlocking Ghost Forms¶

    Evolve select Excellent Bosses into Ghost variants. See Ghost Evolutions for details.

    The Three Sins¶

    Each Sin escalates in difficulty and drops armor upgrades for the next fight.

    Sin of Unreal (First Sin)¶

    Use an Excellent Boss (Giga recommended). Bring Descended Milk for debuff ticks. Straightforward fight.

    Reward: Unlocks the engram for Basic Excellent Boss Armor. Crafting this armor is mandatory for the Sin of Power. Also unlocks Ancient Token Clusters.

    Preparing for Sin of Power¶

    To craft Advanced Excellent Boss Armor, you'll need an Ancient Token Cluster, which requires defeating all 9 Ancients: Bronto, Giga, Raptor, Stego, Wyvern, Carno, Quetz, Rex, and Trike. If not found wild, summon via War Generator.

    Craft your Basic Excellent Boss Armor in the Descend Smithy. Transfer to inventory and find it in your Cosmetic's Tab. Drag the Armor to your dino's costume slot.

    Sin of Power (Second Sin)¶

    Difficulty spike — grueling even with Advanced Excellent Boss Armor.

    Notable Rewards:

    Enhanced Excellent Boss Cube engram — Consumed in your Excellent Boss's inventory to permanently transform it into an Unreal Excellent Boss

    Ancient Token Clusters and Power Spheres — Needed for crafting higher-tier gear

    Once upgraded to Unreal Boss, everything fought up to this point (aside from Combat Bosses) becomes obsolete.

    Unreal Buff Effects:

    • Increased passive dodge chance
    • Dodges trigger powerful regeneration

    Sin of Element (Third Sin)¶

    Requires Unreal Excellent Boss. Less punishing than Sin of Power.

    Rewards:

    Ancient Token Clusters and Element Sphere

    Ghost Cores — Applied to Lightning, Poison, or Flame variants of Wyverns, Drakes, and DodoRexys to transform them into Ghost forms with combined triple-element breath attack

    The Oracle Boss¶

    The Oracle is the capstone of the Excellent Path.

    Summoning Process:

    1. Craft and consume the Oracle Summon in your inventory (not your dino's)
    2. Kill enemies to build experience
    3. At 100,000 XP (check buffs), the Oracle Boss spawns

    Fight Overview:

    • Tank-and-spank boss with sustained DPS
    • At intervals, the Oracle summons minions and becomes immune to damage
    • Clear all minions to remove the shield and resume damaging the boss

    Strong AoE Excellent Bosses (Giga, Indominus Rex, Ice Titan) excel at wiping minions quickly.

    Rewards:

    Oracle Essence — Marks the end of Excellent Boss progression

    Unlocks T3 kibble — Divine and Devilish Kibble now available

    t3-divine-devilish¶

    Divine and Devilish creatures mark a new power level.

    STRUCTURE DAMAGE: N/A

    T3 Progression Paths¶

    Beta Supply Drops — Ancient Token Clusters and resources

    The Infected Line — Dimorph → Ptero → Infected Divine Argentavis

    Legendary Path — Alpha Dodorexy → Alpha Trex Ascend

    See Evolution Paths sections for detailed progression guides: Reaper, Ice Titan, Managarmr, Legendary, Infected, Ancient.

    Additional T3 Encounters¶

    Artifact Boss — Drops Artifacts used in crafting and War Generator summons

    Ascended Poison Drake — T4 battle unlocked at T3, drops soul required for Evolution

    Descended Mega Reaper — Summoned via War Generator, required for Reaper Evolution

    Wild Alpha DodoRexy — Chance to drop Fertilized Legendary Dodo Egg for passive Legendary Essence production

    The Divine & Devilish Divide¶

    Farm Divine and Devilish Souls for T3 Kibble and T4 prep.

    Divine and Devilish Bosses can also appear as rare wild encounters.

    Devilish Phoenix handles both from range with sustained fire damage.

    Divine Guardian — Drops Divine Essence

    Devilish Guardian — Drops Devilish Essence

    Use Divine/Devilish Essence to tame Alpha DodoRexy — a key midgame power jump.

    Defeating both Guardians grants:

    Advanced Flak Armor — Huge survivability boost

    Divine Armor & Devilish Armor — Powerful specialized sets

    Divine & Devilish Armor¶

    These armor pieces can be equipped onto Divine and Devilish creatures:

    100% Melee boost — Stronger defensive scaling

    200% Health boost — Enables creatures to withstand damage from wild Abyssals and Celestials

    This armor is a key stepping stone — without it, T3 creatures can't damage T4 encounters.

    t4-celestial-abyssal¶

    STRUCTURE DAMAGE: N/A

    Equip T3 armor on your Divine/Devilish creatures to begin T4.

    Celestial & Abyssal Hunts¶

    Hunt wild Celestials and Abyssals for Souls, Essences, and Hides.

    Begin with mid-tier Celestials and Abyssals to safely gather materials before targeting elite versions.

    Summon Descended Degenerate Managarmr from War Generator to begin Evolving to Degenerate Form.

    Celestial & Abyssal Boss Encounters¶

    Summon and defeat seven Celestial Bosses and seven Abyssal Bosses.

    Celestial Bosses — Require Divine resources

    Abyssal Bosses — Require Devilish resources

    Each boss unlocks its Evo Core Engram. Craft with Souls to evolve Excellent Bosses into Celestial/Abyssal forms.

    Recommended Strategy: Start with a Devilish Rex, Devilish Phoenix, or Unreal Excellent DodoReaper. Avoid deadly AOE attacks.

    T4 forms handle Element Bosses but lack shields.

    Abyssal Energy and Celestial Energy Farming: Spawn Abyssal Dodoreaper and Celestial Rock Drake for Energy. Both drop 1 Energy Box to inventory plus 6-12 Boxes from lootbags.

    Each boss grants:

    Trophies — Used for Warfare Key recipes and advanced summons

    Souls — Used in Evo Core recipes and Warfare Keys

    All T4 bosses drop Advanced Flak Armor and Large XP Potions.

    Element Bosses¶

    Celestial Element and Abyssal Element are the final T4 challenges.

    Standard Strategy: Army of Abyssal/Celestial creatures with maxed saddles. Abyssal DodoReaper is tankiest.

    Saddle Quality Caps:

    • Most T4 saddles cap at 1,000 armor
    • Celestial Golem caps at ~660 armor
    • Celestial Rock Drake Tek Saddle reaches 1,200 armor
    • Ancient Evo Giga reaches 1,200 armor max

    Recommended Strategy:

    Prep your evolutions first:

    Ancient Line: Craft Evo Core: Ancient Evo Spino, then defeat Ancient Phantom for Ancient Descended Gigant Soul + Celestial Gigant Trophies to evolve Spino to Ancient Evo Giga. Next defeat T4 Celestial Gigant to unlock Evo Core: Celestial Giga. Place Ancient Evo Giga near Celestial Giga and select "Sacrifice" to evolve into Celestial Evo Giga.

    Infected Line: Craft Evo Core: Infected Divine Argentavis, then defeat Descended Infected Wyvern for Infected Celestial Wyvern's Soul + Trophies to evolve into Infected Celestial Wyvern.

    Targeted Boss Counters: Both Descended Infected Wyvern and Ancient Phantom are vulnerable to Purified Evolved dinos.

    Element Boss Tactics:

    Ancient Evo Giga (Melee) — Circle the boss, avoid AoE attacks. When you see "Run Coward," run! Equip max saddle (1,250 armor ideal).

    Infected Celestial Wyvern (Ranged) — Trap the Element boss with pillars or terrain. Bomb from range with Blast ability, weave in fire breaths during cooldown. Expect 2-3 Blast cycles to finish.

    Defeating Element Bosses unlocks Shield engrams and Element Essences.

    Without Shields, survival in Tier 5 is impossible — they're mandatory for damaging and resisting T5 encounters.

    t5-warchiefs¶

    Entry into Warchief Trials and the path toward Havoc/Spectral evolution.

    STRUCTURE DAMAGE: N/A

    T5 Progression Paths¶

    Degenerate Ice Titan — Imbue with Flame or Lightning for siege capability

    Deeply Degenerate Managarmr — High-speed burst-damage flier

    Alpha Supply Drops — Alpha-tier loot runs

    Alpha DodoRexy Evolution — Advance to Alpha T-Rex using Order and Chaos Warchief Souls

    Eternal Light Armor¶

    T5 bosses unlock Eternal Light Armor — higher protection and passive bonuses. Mandatory for Guardians.

    The Warchiefs of Order and Chaos¶

    Equip T4 Shields (mandatory). Use T4 warfighters or Alpha Tyrannosaurus.

    WARNING: The Ancient Evo Giga cannot be used against Warchiefs — it receives a debuff causing it to "cower in fear."

    Alternative Paths¶

    Primordial Reaper Evolution — Evolve your Alpha King or Queen Reaper into the Primordial Reaper.

    Alpha Trex Evolution — Evolve your Alpha DodoRexy into the Alpha Trex.

    Warchief Battle Tips¶

    • Avoid all AOE warnings displaying "Dangerous Attack Incoming"
    • Maintain distance and apply movement discipline
    • Alternate health potions on hotbar and in dino inventory to overlap cooldowns

    Order Warchiefs¶

    Summoned via War Generator Order tab. Each has a unique Scion taming method.

    Eternal Warrior¶

    Unique Drop: Challenger's Eternal Banana (auto-added to inventory)

    Scion Spawn: Scion of Eternal Warrior spawns on death

    Taming Method:

    1. Consume Challenger's Eternal Banana before engaging Scion
    2. Kill Scion — auto-tames at ~10% HP

    Rare Reward: Eternal Sword

    Abilities: Right Click — Rock throw + lightning sphere barrage | C — Melee-ranged lightning AoE | Ctrl — 30s Eternal King Kong power-up

    At low health, avoid DoT-heavy attacks — they can finish the Scion before tame registers.

    Charger, The Tiger Emperor¶

    Scion Spawn: Scion of Tiger Emperor spawns after death

    Taming Method:

    1. Craft Kitty Teaser (Descend Smithy)
    2. Passively feed to Scion — instant tame

    Abilities: Right Click — Conal light attack (stronger under Emperor Buff) | C — Light AoE under buff / Conal unbuffed | Ctrl — 60s Emperor power-up

    Lin, Annihilation Tyrant¶

    Among the most difficult Warchiefs.

    Unique Drop: Annihilation Tyrant Crown (added to inventory)

    Taming Method:

    1. Kill the Tyrant
    2. Craft Summon Tamed Scion at Descend Smithy using the Crown

    Rare Reward: Tyrant Shotgun

    Mode Mechanics: The Tyrant cycles between Beta Mode (Lightning) and Alpha Mode (Flame). Beta has weaker damage but wider AoE. Alpha has stronger damage. Rage meter increases attack speed and elemental trigger chance.

    Chaos Warchiefs¶

    Summoned via War Generator Chaos tab.

    Sariel, The Dying Light¶

    Unique Drop: Challenger's Dying Feather (added to inventory)

    Scion Spawn: Scion of Dying Light spawns on death

    Taming Method:

    1. Consume Challenger's Dying Feather before engaging Scion
    2. Kill Scion — auto-tames at 10% HP

    Rare Reward: Dying Rifle

    Nidhogg, The Galaxy Warchief¶

    The strongest Warchief.

    Preparation: Craft Galaxy Dragon Tribute (Descend Smithy)

    Scion Spawn: Scion of Galaxy Dragon spawns on death

    Taming Method:

    1. Defeat Galaxy Dragon — Scion spawns
    2. Bring to ~20% HP — lands — passively feed Tribute

    Additional Drops: Bionic Essence, Galaxy Essence

    Abilities: Right Click — Chaos flame line AoE | C — Ranged multi-orb attack | Ctrl — 35s Galaxy Lightning Bonus

    Ultimasaurus¶

    Unique Drop: Ultimate Skull (auto-added to inventory)

    Taming Method:

    1. Defeat Ultimasaurus
    2. Craft Summon Tamed Scion at Descend Smithy using Skull
    3. Consume to summon tamed Scion

    Havoc & Spectral Ascension¶

    Descended Reactor Core¶

    Craft in Descended Smithy using Order and Chaos Warchief souls. Enables Havoc/Spectral spawns.

    How It Works:

    Activation: Place in mount's inventory and activate. Killing Celestial/Abyssal creatures spawns Havoc/Spectral variants.

    Duration: The buff lasts indefinitely but is lost if you cryo the dino.

    Cooldown: 600-second reuse cooldown after activation.

    Havoc & Spectral Resource Farming¶

    Farm Havoc and Spectral creatures for:

    Havoc Orbs — For Havoc Kibble and evolution cores

    Spectral Elements — For Spectral Kibble and end-tier blueprints

    Havoc & Spectral Trigger Conversions¶

    Killing certain creatures spawns their Havoc/Spectral variants:

    Abyssal Indominus Rex — Spectral/Havoc Indominus Rex

    Havoc Progression to Ultimate Form¶

    • Kill wild Celestial and Abyssal creatures for progression
    • Boss creatures don't count
    • Higher target HP = more progression value
    • Higher your Havoc's HP = more progression required

    t8-god-trials¶

    STRUCTURE DAMAGE: N/A

    God Trial Requirements¶

    To defeat T8 Gods (Dream, Ascension God / Empty, Descension God), you must have the correct Warchief of the opposite path within scan radius:

    Ascension God (Dream) — Requires a Chaos Warchief nearby

    Descension God (Empty) — Requires an Order Warchief nearby

    Ascension/Descension Boss Mechanics¶

    1. Scan Phase: God spawns and scans your Warchief
    2. Round 1: Summons 1 minion clone of your Warchief (functions like T5 boss but stronger)
    3. Round 2: Scans again, summons 2 clones
    4. Round 3: Scans again, summons 3 clones
    5. Completion: All clones defeated, God is slain

    Killing clones deals damage directly to the God.

    Rewards:

    • Soul of the Ascension God (Dream)
    • Soul of the Descension God (Empty)

    Player Tips¶

    • Use strongest tames — clones are tougher than standard T5 bosses
    • Focus entirely on clones, not the God
    • Keep Warchief within range but avoid aggro
    • Do NOT level up your Warchief before fighting — clone power scales with Warchief level and stats
    • Warchief Reactor boosts also carry over to clones

    Keep your Warchief un-leveled until after defeating the T8 Boss.

    Recommended Strategy¶

    1. Choose your Scion (Tiger Emperor or Dying Light recommended)
    2. Use Warden Reaper or Warden Giga to defeat all three clone waves while keeping maximum distance from the God and Scion
    3. After victory, Descend your Tiger Emperor or Ascend your Dying Light
    4. Use the Essence to evolve your Warden into Supreme Warden form
    5. Supreme Warden becomes your powerhouse for all remaining battles

    The Tyrant Spino is deadliest due to DoT and knockback. Equip Ascended Chibi for 15% damage resistance.

    Ascend / Descend Your Warchief¶

    Once you've collected essences, Ascend or Descend your Warchief by sacrificing the appropriate Warden or Bionic. Your Warchief will have a buff after the boss fight — do not cryo or you lose it.

    Warden Rex — Evo Core feeds to Devilish Rex

    Warden Spino — Evo Core feeds to Divine Spino

    Warden Thorny Dragon — Evo Core feeds to Divine Thorny Dragon

    Warden Therizino — Evo Core feeds to Devilish Therizino

    Warchief Ascension/Descension¶

    Ascension Warchiefs:

    • Dying Light requires Warden Rex
    • Galaxy requires Warden Spino
    • Ultimate Carcha requires Bionic Giga

    Descension Warchiefs:

    • Eternal Warrior requires Warden Therizino
    • Tiger Emperor requires Warden Thorny Dragon
    • Tyrant Spino requires Bionic Giga

    Ascended & Descended Warchief Essences¶

    Every Ascend or Descend grants one corresponding Essence. These are permanent crafting materials not consumed when used — except during final boss evolution.

    Essence Healing Effect: Place in hotbar or creature inventory. Instantly heals 80% HP and Stamina. Ascended Essence only heals Ascended creatures; Descended only heals Descended.

    Ascension is based on total level — spend all unassigned skill points before ascending.

    Summoned via Dark Altar Ritual. Can only be damaged at night.

    Priming the Dark Altar¶

    Before summoning, prime your Dark Altar by loading a Dark Altar Summon Cube and performing seven sequential sacrifices:

    1. Excellent Dino
    2. Unreal Dino
    3. Divine Dino
    4. Devilish Dino
    5. Abyssal Dino
    6. Celestial Dino
    7. Bionic Dino

    Eternal Gorilla — Summons Beast of Oblivion

    Galaxy Warchief — Summons Beast of Desperation

    All sacrifices and summoning must occur at night. The altar must remain in view during the ritual.

    The Battle for Beasts¶

    Bring your strongest T8 creature — Supreme Warden Reaper remains top-tier.

    To Tame: Consume a Challenger's Feather (Dying Light) or Challenger's Eternal Banana (Eternal Gorilla) before engaging. Fight until ~10 HP — auto-tames.

    To Defeat: Engage directly with overwhelming DPS.

    Beast of Darkness¶

    Summoned By: Dying Light

    Taming Method: Consume Challenger's Feather; auto-tames at 10% HP

    Strategy: Performs devastating special attack on 60-second timer. Watch for red glowing orb at center — Dark Core Surge. Hits during channeling increase Dark Multiplier (up to x10). Stop attacking and back away when orb glows. Burst explosion scales with multiplier and can wipe top-tier mounts.

    Drops: Darkness Loot Box containing Fragment of Darkness

    Beast of Oblivion¶

    Summoned By: Eternal Gorilla

    Taming Method: Consume Challenger's Eternal Banana; auto-tames at 10% HP

    Strategy: Watch for "Warning" prompt — signals special tail jab on 60-second timer. If it connects, drains HP, heals itself massively, and gains heavy shield. Dodge immediately when warning appears.

    Drops: Oblivion Loot Box containing Fragment of Oblivion

    Beast of Desperation¶

    Summoned By: Galaxy Warchief

    A massive flying boss with extreme durability and high evasion. Auto-tames upon defeat. Significantly longer fight due to mobility and immense health.

    Strategy: Long tank 'n spank battle.

    Drops: Fragment of Desperation

    Degenerate of Darkness¶

    Seal Type: Fourth and Final Seal (Not tamable)

    Strategy: Towering boss with immense HP and one-shot fireball mechanic. A massive fireball appears above its head for 30 seconds — when 5-10 seconds remain, retreat immediately. The Degenerate roars and erupts, instantly killing anything within aura. Re-engage after blast subsides.

    Drops: Fragment of Desperation, Degenerate Seal Armor (equip to Beast for enhanced defense)

    Key Mechanics & Challenges¶

    • Fight only at night — daylight cancels the challenge
    • Bosses periodically heal for 30M+ HP
    • If multiple tames present, difficulty scales and targeting can glitch

    Always initiate at nighttime. Use open terrain. Expect extended engagements.

    faq¶

    How to Get Luminous Essence¶

    Method 1 — Kill Glow Pets: Slow beginner method. Kill wild glow pets (Bulbdog, etc.) for Luminous Essence and Gems.

    Method 2 — Tame Glow Pets (Recommended): After taming, turn on the glow pet via radial menu. Select "Extract Luminous Essence" — extraction rate is 1 Essence per 100 Charge stored.

    Reapers need lantern pets to fuel Charging Armor. Always keep one active on your shoulder.

    Special Taming Mechanics¶

    Descended DodoWyvern¶

    Requires Excellent Kibble, Grapples + Crossbow. Grapple onto it in open airspace, wait for "Feed Kibble" prompt, feed once then again when prompted. Can swap between Flame, Lightning, and Poison breaths (Ctrl on PC).

    Descended Rock Drake¶

    Summon Rock Drake Emperor via War Generator. Requires 500 Descended Essence, 300 Excellent Essence, 2 Generic Artifacts, 10 Excellent Souls, 25 Rock Drake Feathers. Defeat with Descended DodoWyvern. Loot Fertilized Descended Rock Drake Egg from floor.

    Ghost Evolutions¶

    Excellent Bosses with elemental variants (Lightning, Poison, Flame) can evolve into Ghost forms after defeating Sin of Element.

    Available Evolutions:

    • Ghost Dodorexy
    • Ghost Wyvern
    • Ghost Drake

    To Evolve: Apply Ghost Cores (dropped from Sin of Element) to Lightning, Poison, or Flame variants. Transforms them into Ghost forms with combined triple-element breath attack.

    WARNING: Ghost Evolutions lose their ability to apply torpor.

    Ghost Evolutions deal increased damage against the Oracle Boss.

    Poison Evolutions¶

    Certain Excellent Boss creatures — Poison DodoRexy, Poison Wyvern, Poison Rock Drake — can evolve into powerful forms.

    To Evolve: Feed respective Evo Core (Poison). Requires up to T4 materials including Ascended Poison Drake's Soul (from defeating Ascended Poison Drake via War Generator).

    These evolutions boost damage and resistance, making them top-tier for knockouts.
