    Cataclysm 2.0 — Guide Content¶

    baseline¶

    All stats based on Level 300 unboosted creatures — no server multipliers or custom imprints. Assumes working knowledge of basic ARK mechanics.

    overview¶

    Cataclysm is a dino overhaul mod with influence from classic ARK dino mods. An apocalypse has overtaken the ARK, forcing only the strongest creatures to survive and thrive. This kibble and tier-based overhaul involves killing and breeding for resources.

    The mod starts off moderately difficult — dodos won't hunt you down, making it a great first kibble tamer overhaul. But later tiers become much harder and more of a grind.

    Key Features:

    • 285+ dinos currently in the mod with 300+ more planned
    • Tiered progression from T1 (Runt) to T14 (Ascended)
    • Boss gates every 4 tiers unlock the next phase
    • Fluid-based crafting system replaces traditional poop mechanics
    • Supports all latest DLC creatures

    tier-list¶

    core-mechanics¶

    Tiered Progression¶

    Progress by taming creatures one tier at a time, each requiring crafted kibble.

    Fluid System¶

    Tamed dinos produce "fluids" instead of poop, which are essential for crafting kibble for the next tier.

    WARNING: Fluids only generate if Cataclysm Dino poop is enabled. If using Dino Depot Terminal, ensure poop generation is ON.

    Resource-Locked Taming¶

    You must farm fluids from tamed dinos and collect pearls from wild ones to craft kibble for the next tier.

    Example: To tame a Tidal Rex, kill a Tidal Trike for its pearl, craft Tidal Kibble, then tame the Tidal Rex.

    Boss Gates¶

    Every 4 tiers introduces a boss fight that unlocks the next phase of progression through resources:

    • T4 Cyclonic — Unlocks Glacial Kibble materials
    • T8 Maelstrom — Unlocks Sumerian Kibble materials
    • T12 Watcher — Unlocks Atlantean Kibble materials (Coming Soon)

    Level Requirements¶

    Level 109 required for Tiers 10+. Use Chibi creatures to achieve level 109. Alpha creatures called "Alpha Rex - Avatar" have been added to help with leveling.

    getting-started¶

    Starter Phase (Tier 1 Runt)¶

    1. Play normal ARK — build a small shack, gather basic resources
    2. Kill Runt Dinos on foot
    3. Tame Runt Dino using vanilla mechanics
    4. Use Runt Dino to kill Tidal Dinos and collect Tidal Pearls

    Runt creatures have 1.5x damage and vanilla health — manageable for early game.

    tier-2-tidal¶

    Taming Tidal Creatures¶

    Craft in Player Inventory:

    • Tame Helper — speeds up knockout taming
    • Compound Bow (Level 30) — special Cataclysm bow
    • Arrow 30k — deals 30,000 torpor per hit
    • Tidal Kibble — requires Tidal Pearls + Runt Fluids

    Progression:

    1. Craft Tidal Kibble from collected pearls and farmed fluids
    2. Tame Tidal Dinos with knockout + kibble
    3. Kill Tidal and Rapid Dinos for loot
    4. Set up Tidal Dino farm for Tidal Fluids

    Tidal creatures have 2.5x damage and 2x health.

    tier-3-rapid¶

    Taming Rapid Creatures¶

    Craft:

    • Rapid Kibble — requires Rapid Pearls + Tidal Fluids
    • Arrow 100k — deals 100,000 torpor per hit

    Progression:

    1. Kill Rapid creatures for Rapid Pearls
    2. Farm Tidal Fluids from your tamed Tidal dinos
    3. Craft Rapid Kibble and tame Rapid creatures
    4. Set up Rapid Fluid farm for next tier

    Rapid creatures have 5x damage and 3x health.

    tier-3-5-avatar¶

    Avatars are dinos that remove the player while riding — you become the dino. They function like normal dinos with dino balls, but when you ride them, you play as the creature.

    Taming Avatar¶

    1. Kill Featherlight Avatar and collect Avatar Dust
    2. Craft Avatar Kibble
    3. Tame Featherlight Avatar
    4. Set up Featherlight Avatar Essence farm

    Avatar progression from Runt to Ascended is planned. Currently only Runt Featherlight Avatar is available.

    tier-4-cyclonic-boss¶

    The first major boss gate. Defeating Cyclonic bosses unlocks materials needed for Glacial Kibble (Tier 5).

    Boss Fight¶

    1. Craft Cyclonic Boss Summon in player inventory (search "Boss")
    2. Consume the Cyclonic Boss token
    3. Defeat Cyclonic Boss (Gorilla)
    4. Receive Tameable Cyclonic Boss Token in inventory
    5. Consume token to summon tameable boss

    Boss Tames¶

    Repeat kills to craft all 12 Tameable Bosses, or farm until you have Watcher and Shield Fragment farms going.

    Available Cyclonic Tames:

    • Cyclonic Basilisk
    • Cyclonic Basilosaurus
    • Cyclonic DodoRexy
    • Cyclonic Dragon
    • Cyclonic Gigant
    • Cyclonic Griffin
    • Cyclonic Raptor
    • Cyclonic Rex
    • Cyclonic Rhynio
    • Cyclonic Rock Drake
    • Cyclonic Rock Golem
    • Cyclonic Spider

    Search "Cyclonic" in your inventory to see craftable boss tames.

    tier-5-glacial¶

    Taming Glacial Creatures¶

    Requirements:

    • Watcher Fragments — from Cyclonic Boss kills
    • Shield Fragments — from Cyclonic Boss kills
    • Glacial Pearls — kill wild Glacial creatures

    Craft: Glacial Kibble

    Progression:

    1. Farm boss resources from Cyclonic kills
    2. Kill Glacial creatures for pearls
    3. Craft Glacial Kibble
    4. Tame Glacial creatures

    Glacial creatures have 10x damage and 6x health.

    tier-6-undead¶

    Taming Undead Creatures¶

    Requirements:

    • Glacial Fluids — from tamed Glacial dinos
    • Undead Pearls — kill wild Undead creatures

    Craft:

    • Undead Kibble
    • Arrow 500k — deals 500,000 torpor per hit

    Progression:

    1. Farm Glacial Fluids
    2. Kill Undead creatures for pearls
    3. Craft Undead Kibble and tame Undead creatures
    4. Set up Undead Fluid farm

    Undead creatures have 15x damage and 8x health.

    tier-7-soaring¶

    Taming Soaring Creatures¶

    Requirements:

    • Undead Fluids — from tamed Undead dinos
    • Soaring Pearls — kill wild Soaring creatures

    Craft: Soaring Kibble

    Progression:

    1. Farm Soaring Pearls from kills
    2. Craft Soaring Kibble
    3. Tame Soaring creatures
    4. Farm Soaring Fluids for next tier

    Soaring creatures have 18x damage and 10x health.

    tier-8-maelstrom-boss¶

    The second major boss gate. Defeating Maelstrom bosses unlocks Ancient and Futuristic Fragments needed for Sumerian Kibble.

    Boss Fight¶

    1. Craft Maelstrom Boss in player inventory
    2. Consume Maelstrom Boss token
    3. Defeat the boss
    4. Craft Maelstrom Boss Tame tokens

    Boss Tames¶

    tier-9-sumerian¶

    Taming Sumerian Creatures¶

    Requirements:

    • Ancient Fragments — from Maelstrom Boss kills
    • Futuristic Fragments — from Maelstrom Boss kills
    • Sumerian Pearls — kill wild Sumerian creatures
    • Intelligent Goo — from wild Sumerian kills

    Craft:

    • Artificial Cores — intermediate crafting material
    • Sumerian Kibble — requires Artificial Cores

    Progression:

    1. Defeat wild Sumerian creatures for Pearls and Intelligent Goo
    2. Craft Artificial Cores for Sumerian Kibble
    3. Craft Sumerian Kibble
    4. Tame Sumerian creatures
    5. Farm Sumerian Fluids for Tier 10

    Sumerian creatures have 25x damage and 25x health.

    tier-10-tsunami¶

    Taming Tsunami Creatures¶

    Requirements:

    • Sumerian Fluids — from tamed Sumerian dinos
    • Tsunami Pearls — kill wild Tsunami creatures
    • Genetic Paste — from wild Tsunami kills

    Craft:

    • Genetic Codex — intermediate crafting material
    • Tsunami Kibble — requires Genetic Codex

    New Resources (Tier 10):

    • Tsunami Scales
    • Tsunami Fur
    • Tsunami Hide
    • Tsunami Feathers

    Craft: Tsunami Saddles (Coming Soon)

    Tsunami creatures have 30x damage and 30x health.

    loot-dinos¶

    Special creatures that drop valuable loot when killed.

    Treasure Goat¶

    Drops good ARK loot — armor, weapons, hard-to-gather resources.

    Treasure Stag¶

    Drops Cataclysm loot — weapons, tranqs, bows, potions, summon tokens. Also drops the Tranq Pike.

    Resource Goat¶

    Drops Metal Ingot, Metal, Flint, Stone, Cementing Paste, Polymer.

    Resource Otter¶

    Drops Fish, Prime Fish, Cementing Paste, Polymer, Pearls, Blood.

    Artifact Queen Bee¶

    Drops all artifacts.

    tranqs¶

    Tranq Pike — Unlimited use tranq pike. Drops from Treasure Stag.

    Ranged Tranqs¶

    Compound Bow — Special Cataclysm bow, crafted at level 30.

    Arrow 1M 105 1,000,000

    bosses¶

    Bosses unlock Tek Engrams at Tier 4 Cyclonic and provide materials for continued progression.

    Cyclonic Bosses (T4)¶

    Summon: Craft Cyclonic Boss token at level 50

    Drops: Materials for Glacial Kibble, Tameable Boss Tokens

    Maelstrom Bosses (T8)¶

    Summon: Craft Maelstrom Boss token in player inventory

    Drops: Ancient Fragments, Futuristic Fragments, Tameable Boss Tokens

    Watcher Bosses (T12)¶

    Coming Soon — will unlock Atlantean Kibble materials.

    For all Boss Tames, a side progression where you can upgrade your boss tame outside of the main tier-up progression is planned.

    tek-unlocks¶

    Vanilla ARK Tek Engrams can be unlocked after defeating the Cyclonic Gorilla Boss. Otherwise, use normal map bosses.

    WARNING: Currently bugged due to Wildcard/Devkit issues with rewarding Tek Engrams.

    dino-depot-setup¶

    If using Dino Depot Terminal, log in as Admin and open the Mod Configuration wheel menu.

    Required Settings (Structures Tab):

    • Poop Production Enabled: Yes
    • Poop Interval: 60-180 (recommended)
    • Poop Chance: 1 (whole number, not decimal)
    • Poop Limit: 10,000+ (Cataclysm resources stack)
    • Poop generation requires power: No

    Use a dedicated terminal for Cataclysm Dinos to prevent mixing with other dinos.

    spoil-settings¶

    Set spoil multiplier to 1 or higher — do not make spoil time faster than 1, otherwise players will report fluids and fragments spoiling too fast.

    Special Notes¶

    Other Overhauls: Put Cataclysm 2.0 first in mod order, otherwise only partial spawns may occur.

    Omega Lite: Turn kibble off in admin menu. Do NOT use on first playthrough.

    Insane Everything: Use with CAUTION. Do NOT use on first playthrough.

    faq¶

    How do I craft kibble? Farm fluids from tamed dinos of the previous tier and collect pearls from wild dinos of the target tier. Combine in player inventory.

    Why aren't fluids generating? Ensure Cataclysm Dino poop is enabled. If using Dino Depot, check poop production settings.

    What are Avatars? Dinos that let you play as the creature instead of riding it. Currently only Runt Featherlight Avatar is available.

    Can I craft dinos? Some elusive dinos like Phoenix must be crafted, not tamed wild. Runt Phoenix and Runt Xenomorph are craftable (tier-up not yet available).

    What are Loot Creatures? Treasure Goat, Treasure Stag, Resource Goat, Resource Otter, and Artifact Queen Bee drop valuable items.

    How do I reach level 109 for T10+? Use Chibi creatures. Alpha Rex - Avatar creatures have been added to help with leveling.
